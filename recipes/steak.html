Steak

Pictured is a beautiful medium-rare steak

The recipe for the steak shown above is a really good one that is easy to make and will leave you full.

Ingredients:

  • 1 8 oz. sirloin steak
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Low-sodium soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Honey
  • Dried Italian seasoning
  • Dried mustard

Directions:

  1. Heat a grill to 350-400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Remove your steak from its marinade.
  3. For a medium-rare steak, grill each side for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Use a thermometer to measure the temp of the steak. Steak is done cooking when it is at 130 degrees fahrenheit.
  5. Remove steak from the grill and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle sea salt flakes over the steak.

Lasagna

Pictured is a beautiful homemade lasagna meal

The recipe for the lasagna shown above is a really good one that is easy to make and will leave you full.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground peef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 1 15 oz. container ricotta cheese
  • 15 lasagna noodles

Directions:

  1. Cook ground beef and pork in a skillet on medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic to skillet and cook until golden.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix up the ricotta cheese.
  4. Lay 5 lasagna noodles in skillet
  5. Put beef, pork, and cheese on top of the 5 noodles.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until all noodles are used.

Pancakes

Pictured is a plate of golden brown homemade pancakes

The recipe for the pancakes shown above is a really good one that is easy to make and will leave you full.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 egg

Directions:

  1. Add all dry ingredients into a bowl and mix.
  2. Add all wet ingredients to the same bowl and mix together. You now have the batter.
  3. Scoop batter into a pan or griddle that is cooking on medium-low heat.
  4. Cook batter for 2-3 minutes, then flip, and cook other side for same time.
  5. Remove pancake from pan and add more batter repeating step 4 until you run out of batter.
